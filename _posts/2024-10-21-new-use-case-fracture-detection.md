A new use case is being initiated under the Project AIR framework: fracture detection on X-rays. The project is currently in the preparation phase, as we are working on building a multi-center database for evaluation. Vendors with a fracture detection algorithm will be contacted at a later stage for participation. The protocol is already available <a href="https://marliebesouw.github.io/protocols/">here</a>. Should you have any questions or require further clarification, please do not hesitate to <a href= "mailto:marlie.besouw@radboudumc.nl">contact</a> us.

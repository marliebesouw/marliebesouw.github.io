In January 2024, the first edition of Project AIR was <a href="https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38193833/">published</a> in Radiology by Van Leeuwen et al., comparing the algorithms for Bone Age Prediction and Lung nodule Detection on X-rays. A follow-up study is starting for the latter, including new versionis and new products to update this first round of Project AIR. 

Vendors who participated in this study, that have an updated algorithm in the meantime, can contribute with this follow-up study with their new algorithm. For new vendors is this a new chance to benchmark their algorithm to other algorithms and radiologists. 
Vendors are still welcome to contribute. The protocols can be found <a href="https://marliebesouw.github.io/protocols/">here</a>. If any questions or uncertainties remain, do not hesitate to <a href="mailto:marlie.besouw@radboudumc.nl">contact us</a>.
